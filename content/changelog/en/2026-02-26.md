# v1.3.0 - Accounts, Colors & New List

Create an account, choose your favorite color and see all your countdowns in a refreshed visual.

## What's New

- **Sign in** using Google or a link sent by email
- **Your data migrates automatically** when you sign in — nothing gets lost
- **Choose the app color** with 8 highlight options, saved to your account
- **New grid list** with larger, more visual and clickable cards
- Each card shows a color band indicating event urgency
- Deleting a countdown now has a smooth exit animation
- Floating menu shows your email and sign-out button when signed in

## Improvements

- More accurate day counting (correct rounding)
- Share links load faster
- Settings menu reorganized with clearer sections
