# v1.2.0 - Home Screen, Multiple Events & Notifications

Manage multiple events at once, get reminders and install the app directly on your phone.

## What's New

- **Home screen** with animated icons and a list of all your events
- **Multiple countdowns** — create and track as many events as you want
- **Edit your countdown** at any time, including setting an exact time
- **Notifications** on the event day so you don't forget
- Install the app on your phone's home screen without an app store
- What's New page (you're here!)

## Improvements

- Floating button to switch theme, language and manage notifications
- Smoother animations across all screens
- App version visible in the footer
- Improved layout on smaller screens
- Separators between time units on desktop
