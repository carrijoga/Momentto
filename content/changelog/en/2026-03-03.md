# v1.4.0 - Momentto, SEO & Analytics

The app got its own identity, is easier to find, and we now better understand how you use it.

## What's New

- **Momentto!** The app was renamed from MyTrip to Momentto
- **Preview image** when sharing on WhatsApp, Instagram and social media
- **Sitemap and robots.txt support** for better Google indexing
- **Google Analytics** integrated to understand how the app is used

## Improvements

- Anonymous ID now saved in localStorage — persists across tabs and sessions
- More stable authentication flow when migrating from anonymous to registered account
- Login page with optimized metadata
- More robust authentication callback
