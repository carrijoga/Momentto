# v1.5.0 - Advanced Sharing

Full control over the links you share: choose when they expire, revoke them whenever you want, and save other people's countdowns.

## What's New

- **Choose link expiry** when sharing: 5 days, 30 days, or never
- **Revoke the link** at any time to disable access
- **Save others' countdowns** — when opening a shared link, you can save the countdown to your list
- Quick copy button available in the public view

## Improvements

- Tracking of deletion, notification, and logout events in Analytics
- Database schema updated to support saved countdowns and expiry modes
- Notifications when saving or revoking a shared countdown
