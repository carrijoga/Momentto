# v1.0.0 - Launch

Momentto is here! Create countdown timers for the moments you're looking forward to the most.

## What's New

- **Full countdown** with months, days, hours, minutes and seconds
- **10 categories** for your event: Travel, Wedding, Graduation, Birthday, Party and more
- Progress bar showing how much time has passed
- Confetti when the big day arrives 🎉
- Light, dark or automatic theme
- Available in Portuguese and English
- Works great on mobile and desktop
